Lyricalmyrical Covers

Founder of Lyricalmyrical Press, Luciano Iacobelli published over 100+ Canadian poets over the lifespan of the press. Every Lyricalmyrical book is handcrafted using recycled book covers, dental floss, cloth, and glue.

LyricalMyrical books are not for sale.
