Large Artwork

Luciano's large artwork is primarily crafted using oil on canvas. Drawing from his own experiences growing up in the vibrant Dufferin and St. Clair area of downtown Toronto, the artist's pieces skillfully portray intimate scenes of family life. While on the other hand, Luciano's unbridled imagination also gives life to hauntingly mystical and surreal dreamscapes. The sizes of these works generally range from three to six feet.
