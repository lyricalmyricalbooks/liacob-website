Digital Art

Luciano's digital art, created using the Microsoft Paint program, possesses a unique quality that defies classification. Rarely using colour to depict his forms or his abstractions, Luciano's illustrations emerge in striking monochrome. His digital art appears in his various poetry books, often creatively blending words and images.

Digital art is not for sale.
